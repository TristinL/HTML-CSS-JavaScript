My name is Tristin Lehman. I love Dungeons and Dragons.

Dungeons and Dragons is a TRPG.

Created by Gary Gygax and Dave Arneson in 1974; DnD is now published by Wizards of the Coast and has been since 1997.


You will need a few things if you are thinking about starting a game
  • The players handbook
  • The dungeon masters guide
  • The monster manual (optional)
  • Paper and pencil
  • Some friends and your imagination

Luckily most of these resources are free from the Dungeons and Dragons website located here .

For those of you who would like to learn more about DnD, either as a player or as a dungeon master, feel free to contact me at my email.


Lastly, for those of you who already play and are looking for more resources, a link to my blogger page where I talk about a few resources for homebrewed content.


And remember, if your ever exploring a dungeon, and hear laughter coming from an inanimate object; strike first, ask questions later and never trust a mimic.
